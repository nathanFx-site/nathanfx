Got Questions?
We've got Answers!

Do I need any trading experience to join?

No experience needed. The course is beginner-friendly and starts from the basics, then builds up to advanced strategies.

Do I need a big budget to start trading?

Not at all. You can start practicing on a free demo account or trade live with as little as $50$100. The focus is on skill first, not capital.

What makes this course different from others?

This course teaches you how to think and trade like a real trader, using a proven strategy that combines technical and fundamental analysis. Youll also get real support.

Can I get results even if I work full-time?

Yes. The course is self-paced, and the strategies can be adapted to swing or session-based trading perfect for people with a busy schedule.

What if I dont understand something?

Youre fully supported. You can ask questions directly to our mentor.

Will I learn how to manage risk properly?

Absolutely. The course includes a full module on risk management so you can protect your capital and grow sustainably.

Is the course for lifetime?

Yes, you get lifetime access to the course. Once you enroll, all lessons and materials are yours forever including any future updates.

How long does it take to learn?

That depends on your schedule and experience. Most students complete the course in 1-2 months, but you can go at your own pace. Some start trading confidently even sooner.

Is fundamental analysis needed?

Yes, and thats why its included. Fundamental analysis helps you understand big market moves, news reactions, and long-term trends. Youll learn how to combine it with technical analysis for better results.

Does the course include support?

Absolutely. Youll get 1-on-1 support from our mentor and depending on your package, access to a private community. You’re never left alone.

Can I access the course materials and lessons after completing the course?

Yes youll keep full access to all materials even after you finish. This way, you can review lessons anytime and stay sharp.
